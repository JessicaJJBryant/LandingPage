Who is Sovey?

The Sovey Story starts with all soft, lovey skin but also with a specific grandma: I'm Dipsi: a Mama, grandma, and new great-mama. Then, magically, the opportunity to move to a piece of Kentucky heaven in the Red River Gorge appeared. The little cabin I built among the trees with meadows, woods, and flowing creeks nearby inspired me to expand my herbal healing hobby into a business. Growing, harvesting, wildcrafting, srying, and converting herbs and flowers into healing formulas is a joy. I spend my days doing what I love so that you can be sovey too!
